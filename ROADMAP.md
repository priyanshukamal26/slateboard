# Slateboard Roadmap

This document outlines the planned trajectory for Slateboard development.

## 🟢 Short-term (1–2 Months)
*   **Board Sharing Permissions**: Implement granular control over who can view vs. edit a board (Owner, Editor, Viewer roles).
*   **Enhanced AI (Brainy)**: Integrate more diverse models and improve context-gathering to provide even more accurate academic assistance.
*   **Mobile Web Optimization**: Refine the canvas interaction for touch devices and small screens.

## 🟡 Mid-term (3–6 Months)
*   **Real-time Collaboration Scaling**: Optimize WebSocket namespaces and message batching to support 50+ concurrent users per board.
*   **Folder Organization**: Allow users to group boards into folders/projects for better management.
*   **Rich Media Embedding**: Support for embedding YouTube videos or interactive PDF viewers directly on the canvas.
*   **Template Library**: Pre-built canvas templates for math lessons, chemistry diagrams, and brainstorming sessions.

## 🔴 Long-term (6+ Months)
*   **Native Mobile Apps**: Dedicated iOS and Android applications for a more fluid drawing experience.
*   **Offline Mode**: Enable local drawing and data synchronization once a connection is re-established (using Service Workers and IndexedDB).
*   **Classroom Integration**: Direct integration with LMS platforms like Google Classroom and Canvas for seamless assignment distribution.
*   **Advanced Analytics**: Provide educators with insights into student participation and engagement levels during live sessions.

---

*Note: This roadmap is subject to change based on community feedback and project priorities.*
